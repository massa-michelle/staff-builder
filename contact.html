About the Creators

Michelle Massa

Michelle is a fulltime web developer who loves the outdoors and music too. She received her Bachelor of Science degree in Information Technology and Cyber Security from Wright State University and is currently studying online for her MBA in Digital Technology Management at the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University. She developed the Staff Builder project based on Cara's initial idea and design, and enjoys coding, reading, and writing in her spare time.


Dayton, Ohio. - USA


Cara Clark

Cara is a part-time musician and full-time student pursuing a Bachelor of Arts degree in Music Therapy at the University of Dayton. She receieved her Associate in Music from Sinclair Community College majoring in Voice and minoring in Piano. She thought of Staff Builder while struggling to read music due to undiagnosed dyslexia. She hopes the tool will provide students with better ways to manage their music studies and learning disabilities.


Dayton, Ohio. - USA
