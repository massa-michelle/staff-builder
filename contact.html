About the Creators

Michelle Massa

Michelle is a fulltime developer who loves the outdoors and music too. She developed the Staff Builder project based on Cara's initial idea and design.
She wants a cat.

Cara Clark

Cara is a part-time musician and part-time student pursuing a degree in vocal performance. She thought of Staff Builder while struggling to read music due to undiagnosed dyslexia. She hopes the tool will provide students with better ways to manage their music studies and learning disabilities.
She owns a cat.
